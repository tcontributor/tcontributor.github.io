How to delegate with TezBox online wallet

Step by step instructions

  • Create a new account or login into you web wallet at wallet.tezbox.com
  • Press "Add Account" to create a KT account for delegation.
  • You will need to pay an origination fee - 0.257 XTZ. Press "Yes, continue!" if you have sufficient amount on your TZ account.
  • Press "OK" and wait several minutes for the account to be included on the blockchain.
  • Transfer funds to the newly created KT account by selecting TZ account and using "SEND" tab. Click "SEND" to start transaction.
  • Delegate to Tezos Panda account by selecting KT account and using "DELEGATE" tab. Select "Custom" option, enter our address tz1PeZx7FXy7QRuMREGXGxeipb24RsMMzUNe and then click "UPDATE DELEGATE".
  • Wait a few minutes for the blockchain to reflect the operation.
  • Congratulations and welcome onboard! You are delegating to Tezos Panda now!
